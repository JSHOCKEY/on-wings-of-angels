A Boy Commanded to Run.
A Faith Destined
to Soar.

At just three years old, Alex Sytnyk’s life was torn apart by war. Torn from his mother during the genocidal purges of World War II, he was commanded to run, to survive, and to trust that God and His angels would watch over him.

From orphanages and foster homes across war-torn Europe to eventual refuge in North America, Alex’s story is both harrowing and hope-filled, a testimony to courage, providence, and resilience.
